## **2\. What is Exposition**

**\[Evergreen\]** · Primary visitor: Cold visitor · CTA: continue scroll (no button needed — this section's job is comprehension, not conversion)

Exposition is a student-run industry-engagement platform organised by the Industrial Management Science Students' Association at the Department of Industrial Management, University of Kelaniya. Every year, undergraduates — not a publishing house, not an agency — plan it, report it, and bring in the industry leaders featured in it.

At its centre is Exposition Magazine, running for over 20 years and 20+ issues. Around it sits a calendar of live events — a podcast, an industry forum, a career fair, and a national recognition programme — that connect students directly with the people shaping Sri Lankan business and technology.
